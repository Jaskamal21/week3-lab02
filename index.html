Views from Maldives

Rising tourist destination, 2 March,2024 The Maldives, an archipelago nation located in the Indian Ocean, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, pristine white-sand beaches, and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Comprising 26 atolls and over 1,000 coral islands, it's a paradise for beach lovers, honeymooners, and underwater enthusiasts alike.
